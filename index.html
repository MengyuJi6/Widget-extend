Gintoki

Gintoki Sakata is recognized by his natural wavy hair and sweet tooth. He lives with Kagura and Shinpachi, taking on odd jobs to make the world a better place and to pay their rent.

Shinpachi

Shinpachi regards himself as the comic relief character from the series, but tends to take that as something important. As the readers' perspective, Shinpachi is a pair of glasses that wears a man.

Kagura

Kagura is a young Amanto girl who belongs to the Yato Clan, one of the strongest and most bloodthirsty of the Amanto races. She is also somewhat of a tomboy, as she speaks in a blunt or perverted way.

Sadaharu

Sadaharu chomps on anything smaller than itself, such as Gintoki's and other people's heads. Kagura seems to be the only one who can control him, for she has immense strength.
